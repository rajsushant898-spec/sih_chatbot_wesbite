The Problem We're Solving

In many rural communities, a lack of access to credible health information leads to delayed diagnosis, misinformation, and poor health outcomes. Current digital solutions are often not accessible, lack multilingual support, or require expensive smartphones and stable internet.

Our Innovative Solution: HealthBot

  • Multilingual & Accessible: Built on a robust NLP framework (like Rasa), our chatbot speaks local languages and works seamlessly on WhatsApp and SMS, platforms people already use daily.

  • Real-time & Reliable: We integrate with government health databases to provide accurate, real-time alerts on disease outbreaks, vaccination schedules, and verified health advice.

  • Impact-Driven: Our goal is to achieve 80% accuracy in answering health queries and increase health awareness by 20% in our target communities, as a direct result of this project.
